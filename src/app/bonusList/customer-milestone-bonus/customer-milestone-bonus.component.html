Customer Milestone Bonus :

   This bonus is paid based on the monthly personal customer sales (retail sales), if personal retail sales of a distributor reaches a mile stone (e.g. 25 or 50) then that distributor will be paid with this bonus.

  • The personal retail sales refer to retail sales to 50 different customers.
  • Premium kits do not count for this bonus.
  • The bonus amount is based on the customer club e.g. $100/ month for 25 customers and $200/ month for 50 customers and so on.
