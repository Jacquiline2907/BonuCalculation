Fast Start Bonus :

   As a new representative, a member is eligible for this bonus, if he/she reaches any of the below bench marks in a certain time period.

  • Attaining a distributor position in the organization with in a certain time frame. Eg: If ‘A’ enrols ‘B’ and ‘B’ reaches the position of a qualified sales person in the organization within 30 days, then ‘A’ gets the fast-start bonus.
  • When a new distributor personally enrols 2 customers under him, within their first 30 days, as an enroller of this distributor, the sponsor qualifies for this bonus.

    •    Here the bonus amount may be a fixed amount or fixed percentage and the time to attain bench mark differs based on the company’s requirement

       This is a one-time paid bonus.
