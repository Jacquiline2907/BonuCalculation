Volume Bonus :

    This bonus pays a fixed percentage of bonus for the sales and purchases of the downline to a sponsor.

  • Level restrictions will be applied to this bonus. i.e., the bonus can be paid to the upline only till first manager.
  • To receive this bonus the distributors should qualify certain eligibility conditions e.g. Being active in that month.
  • The bonus % differs based on the current position of both the distributor and the upline member.
