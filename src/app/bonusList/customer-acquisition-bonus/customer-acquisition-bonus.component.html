Customer Acquisition Bonus :

   If a new representative qualifies as a distributor within a certain time, then the sponsor of this distributor is eligible to receive this bonus.

  • The new member should qualify some eligibility conditions for the sponsor to achieve this bonus.
  • The time limit is usually 30, 60 or 90 days.
  • This bonus can be held for certain days from the date the new member shows as being qualified.
  • The bonus % is determined by the leadership position of the sponsor.
