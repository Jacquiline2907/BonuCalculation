Bonuses

Type : Differential Commission

Fast-Start Bonus / Quick start

      As a new representative, a member is eligible for this bonus,if he/she reaches any of the below bench marks in a certain time period.

Personal Sponsored Customer premium kit Bonus

      This bonus is paid to the sponsor when a downline member buys a premium starter kit/ sign up pack or any special kits.

Preferred Customer Sales Bonus

      This bonus can be received by a sponsor or one of his/ her preferred customer’s (a person who is enrolled to the company but not yet attained distributorstatus) purchase.

Volume Bonus

      This bonus pays a fixed percentage of bonus for the sales and purchases of the downline to a sponsor.

Customer Acquisition Bonus

      If a new representative qualifies as a distributor within a certain time, then the sponsor of this distributor is eligible to receive this bonus.

Customer Milestone Bonus

      This bonus is paid based on the monthly personal customer sales (retail sales), if personal retail sales of a distributor reaches a mile stone (e.g. 25 or 50) then that distributor will be paid with this bonus.

Earned-Position-Bonus

      Earned – position bonus can be received by a distributor, when himself or person that distributor personally enrols reaches different levels / ranks of the organization within certain time frame. This is much like Fast-track bonus.

Retail bonus

      The bonus paid can be fixed percentage or it can be a percentage of difference between retail and wholesale price.
